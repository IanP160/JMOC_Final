About Echo

ECHO is the first and only organization dedicated to providing pro bono consulting services to Latino-owned companies from Latino students. We are an organization that fosters both the personal and professional growth of our members, devoted to developing principled business leaders of the future who understand the importance of DEI in the business industry.
